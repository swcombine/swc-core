(defun start
(say "Hi there, how can I help you?")
(add-response "I'm actually looking for a way off this planet." trans)
(add-response "Can you point me to the nearest bar?" bar)
(add-response "You can't. Nobody can." leave)
)

(defun leave
(say "Oh okay then.  Goodbye.")
)

(defun bar
(say "A bar? Oh a tavern. Sorry, I'm not well acquainted with any of those establishments. Best thing to do would be to look around the city yourself, see if you can spot one.")
(add-response "Oh.  Thanks." leave)
(add-response "Can you help me with anything then?" help)
)

(defun help
(say "Sure. I can tell you how to get off this godforsaken rock, but not much else I'm afraid.")
(add-response "I'm actually looking for a way off this planet." trans)
(add-response "Can you point me to the nearest bar?" bar)
(add-response "Oh. Thanks anyway then." leave)
)

(defun trans
(say "Ah. Sure thing. Well there are several methods for it, though some are better than others for different reasons. There is the Transport Network, Commerce, and Organizations. Which would you like to know about?")
(add-response "Transport Network." net)
(add-response "Commerce." commerce)
(add-response "Organizations." faction)
(add-response "Nevermind." leave)
)

(defun commerce
(describe (concat (get-name self) "points out something points out something on your datapad - a link to the " "Commerce section."))
 (say  "Have a look there. The Transportation section has a list of people that are offering transport services. You can also make a post asking for a pickup. However, this area is not that great compared with the others.")
      
(send-message self character "http://www.swcombine.com/forum/forum.php?forumID=52 is the Commerce section link.")
(add-response "Transport Network." net)
(add-response "Organizations." faction)
(add-response "Anything else?" whatelse)
)

(defun faction
(say "There are many organizations out there, and there is a chance you are already with one. Generally they can arrange a pickup for you either using their own guys, or hired help. However, there is a type of organization, the Transport organization, that generally deals with this sort of thing. If you contact them, they should be able to tell you costs and times.")
(add-response "Transport Network." net)
(add-response "Anything else?" whatelse)
)
(defun net
(say "The Transport Network. A fine undertaking, by any standard. If you head to the nearest starport, you can see if it can arrange transport for you. There is usually one starport on each races homeworld that will allow you to travel to any of the other starports on homeworlds. It can also take you to a startport owned by your group.  There are a few other restrictions and such about this form of travel. Best to look at this information flyer.")
(describe (concat (get-name self) " taps your datapad and a link is sent to your mailbox.") )
(send-message self character "http://www.swcombine.com/rules/?NPC_Transport is the Transport Network link.")
(add-response "Commerce." commerce)
(add-response "Organizations." faction)
(add-response "Anything else?" whatelse)
(add-response "Nevermind." leave)
)
(defun whatelse
(say "Not much. If none of those help you, best to ask around see if anyone can help. There are the odd one or two kindly souls who help out. Other than that, not much more I can tell you.")
(add-response "Okay.  Thanks." leave)
(add-response "Can I ask you about transportation again?" trans)
)
